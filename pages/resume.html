How the 2-step tailoring works
01 Extraction pass — AI copies every fact from your resume verbatim into a structured JSON. No generation at this stage.
02 Tailoring pass — AI receives ONLY the extracted JSON (not your raw resume). It selects, reorders, and rewrites in STAR format using only what's in the JSON.
✓ Cannot invent facts — it only has access to what was extracted
✓ Every bullet rewritten in STAR format (Action → Result)
✓ JD keywords mirrored where your real experience supports them
